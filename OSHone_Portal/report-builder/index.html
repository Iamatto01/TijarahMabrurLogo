SCOPE OF MAINTENANCE

# Description Status Remark
1 External Physical condition service: Cleaning all the visual dirt, grease, dust and etc to the satisfied end (as far as practicable).
2 Internal Vessel Service: Cleans all the dirt, oil, water, impurities and etc (as far as practicable). Check for internal corrosion. Touch up with anti-corrosion paint if possible.
3 Safety valve testing: Testing the safety valve functioning manually and in-operation testing at its setting value. Perform calibration check as per TMSB calibration procedure.
4 Draining Valve: Ensure valve is functioning and able to drains the vessel appropriately.
5 Pressure Gauge: 'Zero value' at emptied vessel, marking at MAWP value as designed by manufacturer. Perform calibration check as per TMSB calibration procedure.
6 Testing & commissioning: Running the whole system to ensure all parts and system in good shape & works evidently.
7 Saddle, leg or foundation condition.
8 Nozzle / flange connection and insulation condition.
